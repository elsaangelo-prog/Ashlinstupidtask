Meet our CEO Elsa Angelo Founder & Chief Executive Officer

Elsa Angelo has spent twenty years at the heart of Dubai's most extraordinary property transactions, building not just a business but a legacy of trust, precision, and deeply personal service. Her vision was always simple: to create an agency where clients felt genuinely cared for — not as transactions, but as relationships for life.

How it all began

It started in 2009, in the quiet aftermath of a market storm, when Elsa helped a young family secure their first Dubai home. The gratitude in their eyes that day never left her. She founded The Angelo Collection on a single promise: that every client would feel exactly as that family did — heard, guided, and truly cared for.

Today, that same spirit defines everything we do. As Dubai grows into one of the world's great cities, Elsa's vision reaches further — a future where The Angelo Collection is not just the finest agency in the emirate, but the most trusted name in luxury real estate across the entire region.
